Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn per referral?
You can earn up to ₹3,000 per successful referral. The exact amount depends on the program your referred friend enrolls in. Higher-priced programs offer higher commissions.
When do I receive my referral earnings?
Referral earnings are processed once your referred friend completes 14 days in the program. This ensures they are satisfied with the program before we process the commission.
How can I withdraw my earnings?
Earnings can be withdrawn directly to your bank account. Go to your profile settings, navigate to the "Payments" section, and click on "Withdraw Earnings" to initiate the process.
Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?
No, there is no limit to the number of people you can refer. You can earn unlimited referral income by sharing knowledge with more and more people.
What if my friend forgets to use my referral link?
Your friend must use your referral link or enter your referral code during registration to be counted as your referral. If they forget, they can contact support within 24 hours of registration to add your referral code.
