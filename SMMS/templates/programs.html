Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our programs

Which program is best for beginners?

For complete beginners, we recommend starting with the Complete Trading Program (CTP). This comprehensive program covers all the fundamentals and gradually progresses to advanced concepts, providing a solid foundation for your trading journey.

Do I need prior knowledge to join these programs?

No prior knowledge is required for the Complete Trading Program (CTP) as it starts from the basics. However, for the Arthavidya Mentorship Program (AMP) and Growth Mindset Program (GMP), a basic understanding of trading concepts is beneficial. The Smart Income Program (SIP) is suitable for both beginners and experienced investors.

How are the classes conducted?

All our programs feature live online classes through Zoom, allowing you to interact directly with trainers. Sessions are recorded so you can review them later. We also provide downloadable study materials, assignments, and quizzes to reinforce your learning.

Can I access the course content after completion?

Yes, you will have lifetime access to all recorded sessions and study materials after completing the program. This allows you to revisit concepts and strategies as you continue your trading journey.

What if I miss a live session?

Don't worry if you miss a live session. All classes are recorded and made available in your student dashboard within 24 hours. You can watch the recordings at your convenience and still participate in the next live session.

Do you provide job placement assistance?

Our programs are designed to make you a self-sufficient trader rather than seeking employment. However, top performers may be offered opportunities to become trainers or mentors within our community. We also provide guidance on how to become a professional trader.
