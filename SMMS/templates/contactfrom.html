Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join Arthavidya Trading Community?

You can join Arthavidya Trading Community by enrolling in any of our programs. Visit our Programs page to explore the options and register for the course that suits your needs and goals.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, and bank transfers. You can choose your preferred payment method during the registration process.

Are the classes conducted online or offline?

We offer both online and offline classes. Our online classes are conducted through Zoom, while offline classes are held at our training centers in select cities. You can choose the mode that's most convenient for you.

How can I become a business development associate?

To become a business development associate, visit our Business page and fill out the partner registration form. Our team will review your application and get in touch with you within 2-3 business days.

Do you provide certificates after course completion?

Yes, we provide certificates upon successful completion of our programs. These certificates validate your skills and knowledge in trading and can be a valuable addition to your portfolio.
